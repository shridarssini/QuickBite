About QuickBite

We're on a mission to connect food lovers with the best restaurants, delivering happiness one meal at a time across India's favorite cities.

Our Story

QuickBite started in 2024 with a simple idea — what if finding great food and booking a table was as easy as a single tap? We noticed that food lovers in Chennai, Mumbai, and Bangalore were spending too much time searching for the right restaurant.

So we built QuickBite — a platform that brings together the best restaurants, cafes, and dining experiences in one place. From quick deliveries to premium dine-out reservations, we make every meal memorable.

Today, we partner with hundreds of restaurants across three cities, serving thousands of happy customers every day.

Our Journey

From a small idea to serving thousands of food lovers — here's how QuickBite grew.

January 2024

The Idea Was Born

QuickBite started as a college project — a simple food discovery app for Chennai restaurants.

April 2024

First 50 Restaurants

We onboarded our first 50 restaurant partners in Chennai, covering fine dining to street food.

August 2024

Launched Dine-Out Reservations

Table booking went live — users could now reserve tables at premium restaurants with one click.

December 2024

Expanded to Mumbai

QuickBite went multi-city, bringing Mumbai's iconic food scene to our platform.

March 2025

Bangalore Goes Live

Our third city launch — Bangalore's brewpubs, cafes, and fine dining joined the QuickBite family.

2026 & Beyond

Growing Every Day

500+ restaurants, 25K+ customers, and counting. We're just getting started.
